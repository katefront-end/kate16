Ukrainian & Design
Ukrainian style is very individual, Ukrainian design is literally used everywhere, for example, design in a restaurant, design of a house - apartment, kitchen design and so on.
American & Design
The American style is also very beautiful, but in America there is a different concept of style than in Ukraine. In America itself, their design is more teenage. America adapts more to youth.
Japanese & Design
Japanese style, in China they have a style more like calm, relaxing, Japan likes more minimalism in their country, they make such a calm design in restaurants, hotel houses and so on.
Italian & Design
Italian style, O Italians have a very rich taste, they love luxury in design, they will do rich renovation of the hotel house and so on. They love a lot of gold in design, they have a lot of space to renovate apartment houses and so on.
